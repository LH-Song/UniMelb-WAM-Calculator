# GPA @ UniMelb

 At the University of Melbourne, the GPA (Grade Point Average) is a key indicator of a student’s academic performance. It is calculated based on your grades and the credit points of each subject, playing a significant role in applications for postgraduate programs, scholarships, and employment opportunities. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## How to use the Calculator

 Using this calculator is straightforward:

	1.	Enter Subject Information: Input your grades and the corresponding credit points for each subject.
	2.	Verify Your Data: Ensure all the information you’ve entered is accurate.
	3.	View Results: Click the “Calculate” button to instantly obtain your GPA.
